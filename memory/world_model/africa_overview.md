# Africa — Geopolitical Overview
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Major Active Conflicts
- **Sudan:** SAF vs RSF; ~20,373 killed (2024–2025); escalating drone war (see sudan_civil_war.md)
- **Eastern DRC:** M23/Rwanda effective annexation of Kivus; peace deal unsigned in practice (see drc_eastern_congo.md)
- **Sahel (Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger):** Jihadist insurgencies; regime collapse risk (see sahel_west_africa.md)
- **Somalia:** Al-Shabaab insurgency ongoing; AU mission (AUSSOM) active
- **Mozambique:** RENAMO tensions; Cabo Delgado jihadist insurgency (ISIS affiliate)
- **Ethiopia:** Post-Tigray war tensions; Amhara and Oromo conflicts ongoing

## Great Power Competition in Africa
| Power | Strategy | Key Countries |
|---|---|---|
| Russia/Wagner | Security contracts, mining concessions | Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger, CAR, Libya, Sudan |
| China | BRI infrastructure, mining investment | DRC, Zambia, Ethiopia, Angola, many others |
| USA | Counterterrorism (reduced), minerals deals | DRC, Rwanda, reduced Sahel |
| France | Expelled from Sahel; retreating | Reduced to coastal West Africa |
| UAE | Gulf investment, Sudan RSF backing | Sudan, Somalia, Libya, Horn of Africa |
| Turkey | Arms sales (drones), trade | Somalia, Libya, Sahel |

## Critical Commodities
- **Gold:** Mali, Burkina Faso, Sudan, DRC, Ghana, South Africa
- **Cobalt:** DRC (~70% global)
- **Uranium:** Niger, Namibia, South Africa
- **Oil:** Nigeria, Angola, Libya, Algeria
- **Rare earths:** DRC, Tanzania, South Africa
- **Coltan:** DRC
