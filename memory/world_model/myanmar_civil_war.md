# Myanmar Civil War
**Status: ACTIVE — Junta under sustained pressure**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Current Situation
- Conflict roots: 1948 (world's longest civil war)
- 2021 military coup reignited full-scale violence
- Junta retains major cities but losing countryside to ethnic armed organizations (EAOs)
- People's Defense Forces (PDF) — civilian resistance — active nationwide

## China's Role (Shifting)
- Beijing fears junta collapse → chaos on border
- Xi Jinping met junta chief Min Aung Hlaing
- China supplying military technology: drones, counter-drone equipment
- China backing junta diplomatically while maintaining contacts with some EAOs

## Key Actors
| Actor | Role |
|---|---|
| Military junta (SAC) | Controls cities, formal government |
| Ethnic Armed Organizations (EAOs) | Multiple groups; Brotherhood Alliance dominant in north |
| People's Defense Forces (PDF) | Civil disobedience + armed resistance |
| China | Junta backer; border stability priority |
| ASEAN | Nominal mediator; Five-Point Consensus ignored |

## Commodities Affected
- **Heavy rare earths:** Myanmar = major producer of terbium, dysprosium (critical for magnets, defense)
- **Jade/gemstones:** Junta revenue; largely sanctioned
- **Opium/methamphetamine:** Surging production in conflict zones (Shan State)

## Trajectory
⚠️ GRINDING — Stalemate with junta losing ground slowly; China increasingly interventionist

## Watch For
- Junta collapse in key regions (Mandalay risk)
- China willingness to broker negotiations
- ASEAN cohesion
- Rare earth supply chain disruption signals
