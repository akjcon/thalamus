# Russia–Ukraine War
**Status: ACTIVE — Kinetically live; negotiations stalled by Middle East crisis**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Current Situation
- War entering its fifth year (started Feb 24, 2022)
- Russia occupies ~20% of Ukrainian territory
- Active combat continues despite diplomatic tracks
- Hours before one Geneva session, Russia launched ~420 drones + 39 missiles at Ukraine

## Diplomacy
- U.S.–Ukraine–Russia trilateral talks in Geneva (mid-Feb 2026)
- Two tracks: military and political; military track described as "constructive"
- Previous rounds in Geneva and Abu Dhabi failed
- **Core sticking point:** Russia demands formal control of Donetsk region; Ukraine refuses
- Russia has not signaled acceptance of foreign troops inside Ukraine as peacekeepers
- **NEW RISK:** U.S. attention consumed by Middle East regional war; Ukraine negotiation momentum may stall

## Security Guarantees
- UK and France pledged "military hubs" in Ukraine post-peace deal
- USA backs security guarantees + truce monitoring mechanism leadership
- "Coalition of the Willing" — 35 countries engaged

## Russia's Strategic Position vis-à-vis Middle East Crisis
- Russia not directly involved in Iran war
- **Potential beneficiary:** Energy prices spiking; oil revenues increasing
- May leverage crisis to seek sanctions concessions from energy-desperate Europe
- Western attention divided; Ukraine pressure on Russia may ease temporarily
- Russia–Iran alignment: Moscow watching IRGC situation carefully

## Commodities Affected
- **Grain:** Ukraine top-5 exporter of wheat, corn, sunflower oil; Black Sea corridor contested
- **Steel/Iron ore:** Ukrainian exports severely disrupted
- **Energy:** Russian oil/gas under sanctions; Russia benefits from Middle East price spike

## Trajectory
⚠️ FROZEN/STALLING — Talks deadlocked on Donbas; Middle East crisis diverts U.S. attention; Russia may see opportunity in Western distraction
