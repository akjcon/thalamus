# Sudan Civil War
**Status: ESCALATING**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Current Situation
- War between Sudanese Armed Forces (SAF) and Rapid Support Forces (RSF)
- Started: April 2023
- ~20,373 killed Aug 2024–Aug 2025 (one of world's deadliest conflicts)
- Early 2026: Fighting intensified in Kordofan — daily drone strikes on civilians, markets, hospitals

## External Backers
- **RSF backed by:** UAE (financing, weapons); Ethiopia hosting secret RSF training camp (Reuters, Feb 2026)
- **SAF aligned with:** Darfuri armed groups, Islamist factions
- Saudi Arabia and UAE tensions hampering ceasefire efforts
- Egypt backing SAF

## Mediation
- SAF allies rejecting negotiations with RSF
- UAE–Saudi rivalry fracturing external coalition
- U.S. engagement limited

## Key Actors
| Actor | Role |
|---|---|
| SAF | Sudanese military government |
| RSF | Paramilitary (Gen. Dagalo/Hemeti) |
| UAE | Primary RSF backer |
| Egypt | SAF backer |
| Saudi Arabia | Nominal mediator, tensions with UAE |
| Ethiopia | Alleged RSF training host |

## Commodities Affected
- **Gold:** RSF funds operations via gold exports to UAE; mining disrupted
- **Agriculture:** Sudan is regional grain/sesame exporter; food supply severely disrupted
- **Humanitarian:** Aid corridors critically strained; famine risk

## Trajectory
🔴 ESCALATING — Drone warfare intensifying; mediation failing; proxy war deepening

## Watch For
- UAE–Saudi tensions fracturing backer coalition
- Spillover into Chad and Ethiopia
- U.S. new Sudan policy engagement
- Famine declaration thresholds
