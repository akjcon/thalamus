# Europe — Security Posture & Defense
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Core Dynamics
- Europe facing pressure to increase defense spending amid U.S. reliability questions
- UK and France leading "Coalition of the Willing" for Ukraine security
- NATO cohesion under stress but holding
- EU deepening defense industrial cooperation
- **NEW: Energy emergency scenario re-emerging due to Qatar LNG halt and Hormuz threat**

## Ukraine Support
- UK and France pledged "military hubs" in Ukraine contingent on peace deal
- 35-country "Coalition of the Willing" formed to backstop Ukraine security
- EU continuing financial support (multi-year package)
- EU 20th sanctions package against Russia in progress
- European defense spending rising: multiple NATO members now at/above 2% GDP target

## Energy Security — RE-EMERGED CRISIS
- Europe had successfully pivoted from Russian pipeline gas to LNG (U.S., Norwegian, **Qatari**)
- **Qatar LNG now halted** — removes one of Europe's two primary LNG pillars
- No immediate substitute at Qatar's volume (~20% global LNG supply)
- EU emergency energy measures expected imminently
- Options under consideration:
  - IEA coordinated strategic reserve release
  - Emergency demand reduction mandates
  - Potential pause on Russian LNG sanctions enforcement (politically toxic)
  - Accelerated U.S. LNG import contracts (medium-term only)
- 2022-style energy crisis scenario has re-emerged

## EU Institutional Developments
- EU Critical Raw Materials Act implementation
- EU defense industrial strategy (EDIS)
- 20th Russia sanctions package: Anti-circumvention tool activated (Kyrgyzstan)
- **New priority:** Emergency energy security response to Qatar halt

## Trajectory
🔴 ENERGY EMERGENCY / 🟡 DEFENSE ADAPTING — Energy crisis re-emergence is primary near-term European risk; defense posture improving but slow
