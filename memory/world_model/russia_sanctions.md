# Russia Sanctions Regime
**Status: ESCALATING — Full-spectrum, shadow fleet targeted**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Key Recent Actions
- **Oct 2025:** U.S. sanctions on Rosneft and Lukoil — most significant energy sanctions escalation since 2022
- **Feb 2026:** EU 20th sanctions package (expected adopted late Feb 2026)
  - Full maritime services ban for Russian crude oil
  - Harder to acquire oil tankers
  - Bans on maintenance/services for Russian LNG tankers and icebreakers
  - **First use of "anti-circumvention tool":** Prohibits exports of certain goods to high-risk re-export jurisdictions
  - **Kyrgyzstan** targeted for anti-circumvention sanctions
- EU 18th package already targeted shadow fleet vessel managers and customers

## Russian Oil Export Landscape
- ~95% of Russian crude exports go to: **India, China, Türkiye**
- Shadow/dark fleet actively enables sanctions evasion
- Western enforcement increasingly targeting third-country enablers

## Sanctions Scope (Cumulative)
- Energy: oil, gas, LNG
- Finance: SWIFT exclusions, asset freezes
- Technology: dual-use goods, semiconductors
- Individuals: oligarchs, government officials
- Shipping: shadow fleet targeting
- Metals: steel, aluminum, gold
