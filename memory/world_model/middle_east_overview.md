# Middle East — Regional Overview
**Status: 🔴 ACTIVE REGIONAL WAR**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## SITUATION SUMMARY
The Middle East is in active multi-state war. Iran's leadership has been decapitated; interim council confirmed but lacks constitutional mandate and IRGC command authority. IRGC is operating autonomously under Vahidi. Iran is attacking 9 countries and 27 U.S. bases. Lebanon ceasefire is over. Both Hormuz and Red Sea are under simultaneous attack. Qatar LNG halted.

## Country-by-Country Status

### Iran
- Khamenei **DEAD** — interim council confirmed: Larijani (SNSC), Pezeshkian (President), Mohseni-Ejei (Judiciary)
- **Larijani is de facto crisis manager** but constitutionally ineligible for Supreme Leader; U.S.-sanctioned Jan 15, 2026
- **IRGC on autonomous protocols** under Vahidi — rival power center to civilian council
- Attacking 9 countries + 27 U.S. bases across Middle East
- Diplomacy: FM signals Oman back-channel openness; Trump confirms talks possible
- Nuclear program: No confirmed strikes on nuclear installations (IAEA)
- Assembly of Experts must convene to elect new Supreme Leader — timeline unclear in wartime
- See: [iran_regional_war.md](iran_regional_war.md) | [iran_succession.md](iran_succession.md)

### Israel
- Conducting multi-front operations: Lebanon (active), Syria (strikes), Iran (strikes done)
- Gaza ceasefire still nominally holding
- Hezbollah leader marked for elimination; IDF warns of 'long campaign' in Lebanon
- Defense minister driving escalation posture

### Saudi Arabia 🔴 POSITION SHIFT
- Under Iranian missile/drone attack
- **Publicly condemned Iranian attacks on Gulf states** but did NOT condemn U.S.–Israeli strikes on Iran
- **State media expressing hope for Iranian regime collapse** — significant position signal
- Aramco infrastructure at direct strike risk (largest domestic refinery shuttered)
- OPEC+ decision-making paralyzed by active conflict
- **Core fear:** Iranian *state* collapse (not just regime change) would be destabilizing — wants weakened-but-functional Iran, not failed state
- **Divergence from UAE:** Saudi fears Israeli post-Iran hegemony; UAE seen as more closely aligned with Israel

### UAE 🟡 DIVERGING FROM SAUDI
- Under Iranian missile/drone attack
- Energy infrastructure at risk
- Seen as aligning more closely with Israel than Saudi Arabia
- **Saudi–UAE structural tension:** UAE's Israeli alignment vs. Saudi's fear of Israeli regional hegemony post-Iran
- Gulf states' shared fear: Iranian state collapse = Hormuz instability, Shia proxy collapse, refugee flows, failed-state chaos

### Qatar 🔴 NEW
- Under Iranian missile/drone attack
- Downed 2 Iranian fighter jets; intercepted 165 missiles + 541 drones
- **LNG production halted** — see [qatar_crisis.md](qatar_crisis.md)
- Al Udeid Air Base (U.S. CENTCOM) at risk
- Mediator role fully suspended

### Kuwait / Bahrain / Oman / Iraq / Jordan
- All under Iranian missile/drone attack
- Oman: Active diplomatic channel for Iran back-channel (traditional role); itself under attack — paradox
- Bahrain: Hosts U.S. 5th Fleet
- Iraq: Significant U.S. base presence targeted

### Lebanon
- Ceasefire **CONFIRMED OVER** as of March 2, 2026
- Hezbollah fired rockets at Israeli missile defense (Mishmar al-Karmel)
- Israeli strikes on Beirut resumed
- IDF evacuation orders: 50 southern Lebanon + Beqaa Valley villages
- Lebanese cabinet: voted to outlaw Hezbollah military activity; ordered arrests
- Elections May 2026: now in extreme jeopardy

### Yemen / Houthis
- **Red Sea attacks RESUMED** — Iran conflict as new justification
- Capability intact; will persist through Iran conflict

### Syria
- Post-Assad al-Sharaa government; rebuilding
- Israel continuing strikes on Syrian military infrastructure
- Position in regional war: unclear/passive

### Turkey
- NATO member; watching from sidelines

- Buying Russian oil; interests in regional stability
