# Middle East — Regional Overview
**Status: 🔴 ACTIVE REGIONAL WAR**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## SITUATION SUMMARY
The Middle East is in active multi-state war. Iran's leadership has been decapitated; IRGC is operating autonomously; Iran is attacking 9 countries and 27 U.S. bases. Lebanon ceasefire is over. Both Hormuz and Red Sea are under simultaneous attack. Qatar LNG halted.

- Nuclear program: No confirmed strikes on nuclear installations (IAEA)
- See: [iran_regional_war.md](iran_regional_war.md)

### Syria

### Turkey
