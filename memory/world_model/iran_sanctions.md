# Iran Sanctions — SUPERSEDED BY ACTIVE WAR
**Status: SEE [iran_regional_war.md](iran_regional_war.md)**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

The sanctions framework that governed Iran relations through early 2026 has been overtaken by active military conflict. Pre-war context below for reference.

## Pre-War Sanctions Context
- U.S. maximum pressure; OFAC enforcing against third-country enablers
- EU full sanctions + IRGC terrorist designation (Jan 29, 2026)
- Iran exporting ~1.5–2 mb/d crude, primarily to China via shadow fleet
- Post-June 2025 Israel–Iran war: Iran rebuilding military + nuclear capabilities

## Current Sanctions Relevance
- Active war has effectively suspended normal sanctions enforcement discussions
- Iranian oil exports to China disrupted by Hormuz closure threat and conflict atmosphere
- Sanctions architecture likely to be restructured post-conflict based on outcome
