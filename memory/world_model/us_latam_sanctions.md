- **Cuba pharmaceutical sector at risk:** Cuban biotech/pharma (Interferon, cancer vaccines) used across Latin America — energy collapse stops production; Latin American healthcare systems exposed

- **Migration surge risk:** Cuba collapse timeline has accelerated to a 2026 scenario; Florida migration surge is now plausible within 2026, not hypothetical
