# Qatar — Under Attack; LNG & Ammonia Production Halted
**Status: 🔴 ACTIVE CRISIS — Belligerent target; global LNG + nitrogen shock**
**As of: March 2, 2026**

## Situation
- Qatar under **Iranian missile and drone attack** as part of regional war
- Qatar **downed two Iranian fighter jets**
- Qatar air defenses intercepted **165 ballistic missiles + 541 drones** (as of March 2)
- **QatarEnergy halted LNG production** — largest single LNG supply shock in modern history
- **Qafco (Qatar Fertiliser Company) ammonia and urea exports also halted** — direct nitrogen supply disruption
- **Al Udeid Air Base** (largest U.S. base in Middle East; ~10,000 personnel) potentially targeted

## LNG Impact — CRITICAL
- Qatar = world's largest LNG exporter (~77 million tons/year; ~20–22% of global LNG supply)
- Production halt = **~20% of global LNG offline**
- Primary customers affected: EU (heavily dependent post-Russia pivot), Japan, South Korea, India, China
- European energy emergency scenario confirmed re-emerged
- **Physical restart minimum: 3–6 weeks** regardless of ceasefire timing — liquefaction train recommissioning, safety inspections required
- No immediate alternative at this volume; spot LNG prices spiking severely

## Ammonia/Urea Impact — COMPOUNDING FERTILIZER CRISIS
- Qafco is a **major global ammonia and urea exporter**
- Production halt removes significant volume from global nitrogen markets
- This is the **direct Middle East supply disruption channel** for nitrogen fertilizers
- Compounds the European factory curtailment channel (TTF above threshold)
- Together: structurally worse than 2021 analog
- See [fertilizer_agricultural_inputs.md](fertilizer_agricultural_inputs.md)
